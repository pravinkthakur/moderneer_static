Maturity Model

A self‑serve assessment across 10 dimensions. Output is a score, narrative, and a focused plan. Great Agile teams often peak around level 3; Outcome Engineering extends beyond.

Dimensions

  • Strategy‑Outcome Link
  • Product Discovery
  • Engineering Excellence
  • Shift‑Left Quality
  • Platform & Enablement
  • Data & Observability
  • Operational Excellence
  • Security & Compliance
  • Customer Connection
  • Feedback Loops

Levels

LevelDescriptorSignal
1Ad‑hocHeroics, unclear outcomes
2DefinedProcesses exist, outcomes fuzzy
3PerformingConsistent delivery, partial outcome link
4Outcome‑DrivenTelemetry‑led, reliable, secure
5OptimisingContinuous learning at scale

Distribution assumption: ~50% ≈ 1.5, ~20% ≈ 2.5, ~5% between 2.5–3, and ~5% > 3.

Safety note: Assessments avoid client names and sensitive details. Reports share themes and anonymised metrics.
